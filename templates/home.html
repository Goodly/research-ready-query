Research Ready Archives is a project of Goodly Labs, which has converted the static, difficult-to-research Congressional Record and Hearings Transcripts into a well-organized, easy-to-query database linking floor speeches and committee hearing minutes to data describing Members of Congress and their constituencies.

After many hundreds of hours of volunteer effort and modest support from the Social Science Research Council, Research Ready is now enabling a wave of inquiry into Congressional Members’ utterances on Capitol Hill. With this open resource, researchers, journalists, and the public are now able to easily ask and answer powerful questions revealing the dynamics of Congress Members’ interactions, shifting legislative priorities, the interplay between electoral and governing incentives, the difference between what political actors say and do… and much more.
